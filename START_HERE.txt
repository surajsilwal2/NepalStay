  👤 Nationality System

    • FOREIGN users must provide passport & purpose of visit

    • Example: foreign@example.com registered as FOREIGN with GB123456 passport

  🛏️  Unique Room Management
    • Hotel 1: 8 unique rooms (101, 102, 103, 104, 201, 202, 203, 204)
    • Hotel 2: 32 unique rooms (B01-B08, 101-108, 201-208, 301-308)
    • Each room has unique number AND unique name per hotel
    • Database constraints enforce uniqueness

  🏨 Hotel Classification
    • SMALL: Urban Kathmandu Boutique (8 rooms, no staff management)
    • LARGE: Pokhara Mountain Lodge (32 rooms, staff management enabled)
    • Classification: SMALL (<12), MEDIUM (12-30), LARGE (30+)
    • staffEnabled flag based on room count

  🔒 Data Isolation
    • Vendors see only their own hotel & rooms
    • Staff see only their assigned hotel
    • Customers see only their bookings
    • Admin sees everything
    • Enforced at API level with session.user.id checks
